# AI Usage Declaration

In accordance with the module's reuse and AI usage policies, our team explicitly declares the following uses of Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools during the development of MediTrack:

### Tools Utilized
* **Large Language Models (LLMs):** Google Gemini / Perplexity
* **UI/Visual Assistants:** Google Stitch MCP and Antigravity

### Scope of AI Usage (Adhering to "Good Use" Guidelines)
1. **UI Boilerplate & Autocomplete:** Google Stitch MCP and Antigravity were used to assist with the rapid prototyping and autocompletion of JavaFX layout structures (e.g., `VBox`, `HBox`, `GridPane`) and applying CSS color-theming.
2. **Debugging Help:** LLMs were used as an interactive debugging tool to help diagnose asynchronous JavaFX thread synchronization issues and to identify indexing discrepancies between visual tables and backend lists.
3. **Learning Alternatives:** AI was used to explore alternative formatting for Markdown tables, generate standard PlantUML script syntax for architecture diagrams, and proofread documentation.
4. **Media & Presentation (Demo Video):** An AI Text-to-Speech tool was used to generate the audio voiceover for our final project demonstration video. The audio was generated strictly based on original subtitles and a script manually written by our team.

### Statement of Responsibility and Verification
We strictly adhered to the policy against using AI to generate full solutions. We acknowledge the warning that AI-generated code can be incorrect or flag plagiarism checkers.

Therefore, all AI-assisted boilerplate and debugging suggestions were explicitly reviewed, manually verified, and heavily refactored by our team to fit our specific architecture, RBAC implementation, and code quality standards. Furthermore, all AI-generated media (voiceovers) reflect our team's original content and scripts. We take full responsibility for the accuracy, functionality, and originality of the final submitted application and deliverables.
